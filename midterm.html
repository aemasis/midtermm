About

Friends of Palo Alto Library are proud to present a monthly book sale of over 50,000 wonderful used books, records, videos, magazines, and more. Support the library by purchasing our low-priced books from the nicest booksellers around. 100% of proceeds go to the Palo Alto Library.

Sale Policies

Twelve Book Policy

We have a sale policy of no more than twelve books per shopper before noon, due to the high number of shoppers. The limitation continues after noon if there is still a line outside. Once there is no longer a line outside, shoppers may purchase more than twelve books.

Line Policy

The sales line operates on a numbered ticket system. Shoppers may begin to line up at 8 am to receive their tickets, which hold their spot in line, enabling them to leave the sale and return when the doors open. There is a two ticket per person limit.
